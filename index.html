RDFIA - Master DAC - Sorbonne University

This page contains the practical sessions done for the RDFIA course.

The practical sessions are supervised by Nicolas Thome (nicolas.thome@sorbonne-universite.fr), Guillaume Couairon (guillaume.couairon@gmail.com), Asya Grechka (aagrechka @gmail.com), and Mustafa Sukhor (mustafa.shukor@isir.upmc.fr).

To simplify our job, send all emails to the supervisor of your group and the email object must be [RDFIA][Homework-*] with the * indicating the homework identifier (1, 2, or 3). If it's not related to a homework, please use [RDFIA][MISC].

Send your homework in ENGLISH please!
