RDFIA - Master DAC - Sorbonne University

This page contains the practical sessions done for the RDFIA course.

The practical sessions are supervised by Nicolas Thome (nicolas.thome[at]sorbonne-universite.fr), Guillaume Couairon (guillaume.couairon[at]gmail.com), Asya Grechka (aagrechka[at]gmail.com), and Mustafa Shukor (mustafa.shukor[at]isir.upmc.fr).
Each homework must contain:

  • A report (pdf file) containing:
    • A general summary of the problem, the context, and the approach that we tackled in these TPs.
    • The answers to all questions (with mark * ou without mark)
    • The answers to questions with marks **, corresponding to analyzing and discussing your results
  • The source code (.ipynb file) of your work
Your answers should be concise and to the point. Include graphs and images that support your arguments.

All homeworks must be sent to the address tp.rdfia@gmail.com. The email object must be [RDFIA][group-x][Homework-*] with the * indicating the homework identifier (1, 2, or 3). If it's not related to a homework, please use [RDFIA][MISC].

  • Groupe 1: 14/15 501 (First TP session with Nicolas Thome)
  • Groupe 2: 14/15 502 (First TP session with Asya Grechka)
  • Groupe 3: 14/15 407 (First TP session with Guillaume Couairon)

Send your homework in ENGLISH please!
